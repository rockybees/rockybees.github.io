Prime factorization of 12 using continuous division:

\[
\begin{array}{c|c}
    2 & 12 \\
    2 & 6  \\
    3 & 3  \\
      \hline
      & 1  
\end{array}
\]

Thus, the prime factorization of \(12\) is \( 2^2 \times 3 \).





 using continuous division:

\[
\begin{array}{c|c}
    2 & 12\ \ \ 16\\
    2 & 6\ \ \  8\\
          \hline
       & 3\ \ \  4\\
\end{array}
\]

Thus, the prime factorization of \(12\) is \( 2^2 \times 3 \).
